Your Data, Your Control

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our Promise: Your data never leaves your browser

We don't collect, store, or transmit your prompts to any server. Everything you type stays 100% local on your device. Period.

01

Secure Data Sync

PromptEnhance provides an optional Secure Account feature. When you sign in with Google or Email, your prompt history is synchronized across your devices using encrypted cloud storage.

Your prompts are still processed entirely within your browser or via our secure server proxy. We do not use your data to train AI models or share it with advertisers.

02

Local Storage Only

To provide the Prompt History feature, we use your browser's built-in localStorage. This means:

  • Your prompt history is stored only on your device
  • No one else can see it — not us, not anyone
  • It stays in your browser until you choose to clear it
  • If you clear your browser data, the history is permanently deleted
  • Each browser/device has its own separate history

We never sync, upload, or back up your history to any cloud service.

03

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting technologies. There is:

  • No Google Analytics
  • No Facebook Pixel
  • No session recording
  • No behavioral tracking
  • No advertising cookies

When you leave the site, we have zero record that you were ever here.

04

No Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to share. We will never:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your prompts with advertisers
  • Use your input to train any AI model
  • Provide data to data brokers or marketing companies
  • Hand over information to any entity (there is none to hand over)
05

Cookies

PromptEnhance does not use cookies. We use localStorage solely for your prompt history feature, which is entirely under your control.

If ads are introduced in the future, Google AdSense may use cookies. We will update this policy and clearly notify users before any such change.

06

Your Control

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • View your history — Visit the History page anytime
  • Delete individual prompts — Remove any single entry from your history
  • Clear all history — One click to erase everything
  • Browser controls — Clearing your browser data also removes all stored history
07

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. We are committed to maintaining the same level of privacy protection.

Questions? Reach us at proprompterr@gmail.com.

Your prompts. Your data. Always.
